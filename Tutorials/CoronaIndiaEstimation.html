Corona Growth Estimation for India (Naive estimation)¶

This experiment is to plot the data and find an approximate growth factor. Stupidity of people/government is not taken into consideration. Hence, this estimation doesn't include the possibility Social Gatherings.¶

1-->2-->3-->5-->6-->29-->30-->31-->34-->39-->45-->50-->71-->76-->83-->95-->109-->120-->141-->171-->202 is the list of +ve corona cases daily from Jan 30th to March 19th.
