Find the rate of change of growth¶

Watch this extraordinary video https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kas0tIxDvrg to gain a mathematical perspective of the situation.

Video states that No pandemic strictly follows an exponential growth but subsides after a certain point. This graph levels out when it comes to the size of population and is referred as Logistic Curve. And that certain point prior to decrease in growth is referred to as Inflection Point. Gradual decrease in the Growth factor means that the slope of growth is decreasing.

Calculating the Growth Factor is also mentioned in the video:¶

In [23]:

In [25]:
growth_factors = [changes[i+1]/changes[i] for i in range(len(changes)-1)]
growth_factors

For India to reach the inflection point, the blue plot should go low beyond the orange line i.e the Growth factor <1 and is steadily decreasing. You may see a few points where blue plot is crossing the Orange line, but it too early for us to say we are beyond inflection point. Remember that the logistic curve works at the size of population. This is too early to be an indicator.¶
