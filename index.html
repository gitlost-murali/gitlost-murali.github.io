Majoritarianism & Dissent:

When I was reading the book 1984, I came across a concept called Thought Police. Thought Police: Where everything (devices, people) around you starts to dissect & scrutinize your dissent for the existing rule. While the book is setup in an dystopian world, what I worry for, is the Thought Police in current times. Imagine you criticize an establishment and start worrying about the citizenship score.
